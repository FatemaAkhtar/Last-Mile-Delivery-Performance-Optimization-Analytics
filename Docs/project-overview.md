# 🚚 LogisticsIQ – Enterprise Business Intelligence Platform

## Overview

LogisticsIQ is an enterprise Business Intelligence solution designed to transform large-scale logistics operations into actionable business insights. The platform centralizes operational, geographical, and time-based analytics into a unified reporting experience, enabling stakeholders to monitor network performance, identify service bottlenecks, and support data-driven operational decisions.

---

# Business Challenge

Modern logistics organizations process millions of pickup and delivery transactions across multiple cities and service regions every month.

Traditional reporting methods often make it difficult to:

* Monitor operational performance in real time
* Compare regional service quality
* Identify delay hotspots
* Track Service Level Agreement (SLA) compliance
* Analyze operational trends across geography and time

A centralized analytics solution was required to provide executives and operations teams with a single source of truth.

---

# Solution

LogisticsIQ was designed as a multi-page Business Intelligence platform that combines executive reporting, operational monitoring, geo intelligence, and timing analytics into one interactive experience.

The solution leverages dimensional data modeling and business-driven visualizations to simplify complex logistics data and improve operational visibility.

---

# Solution Architecture

### Data Model

The reporting solution is built on a dimensional model consisting of:

* **2 Fact Tables**

  * Pickup Transactions
  * Delivery Transactions

* **5 Shared Dimension Tables**

  * City
  * Region
  * Courier
  * Area of Interest (AOI)
  * Date

This architecture enables consistent filtering, scalable reporting, and cross-functional analysis across multiple dashboard pages.

---

# Dashboard Modules

## Executive Dashboard

Provides a consolidated overview of operational KPIs, delivery performance, and network health.

### Operations Control Center

Monitors operational volume, delay hotspots, city performance, and service efficiency.

### Geo Intelligence Center

Visualizes regional performance through interactive maps, SLA monitoring, and location-based operational insights.

### Operational Timing Intelligence

Analyzes pickup and delivery patterns across different time periods to identify peak activity windows and operational trends.
