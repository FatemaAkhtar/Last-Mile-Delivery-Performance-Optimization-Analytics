# 📌 Business Challenge

Logistics organizations generate millions of pickup and delivery transactions every month across multiple cities, couriers, and service regions.

Despite the availability of operational data, decision-makers often struggle to answer critical business questions:

* Which regions consistently miss SLA targets?
* Where are operational delays concentrated?
* Which cities require immediate attention?
* How do pickup and delivery trends change over time?
* How can multiple operational reports be unified into a single executive view?

Without a centralized analytics platform, operational monitoring becomes fragmented, making proactive decision-making difficult.

---

# 💡 Solution

LogisticsIQ was designed as a multi-page Business Intelligence platform that transforms raw operational data into interactive executive dashboards.

By combining dimensional data modeling, business-focused KPIs, geo intelligence, and operational analytics, the solution provides a single source of truth for monitoring logistics performance.

# 🏗 Solution Architecture

The reporting solution follows a dimensional modeling approach for scalable and consistent analytics.

## Data Model

* **2 Fact Tables**

  * Pickup Transactions
  * Delivery Transactions

* **5 Shared Dimensions**

  * City
  * Region
  * Courier
  * Area of Interest (AOI)
  * Date

This architecture enables reusable business dimensions, efficient filtering, and consistent cross-dashboard reporting.
